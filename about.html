About the Team

Getting to know the Ben Avery Clay Crushers

Our Mission

The Ben Avery Clay Crushers is a non-profit 501©3 volunteer youth development program based at the Ben Avery Clay Target Center in Phoenix, Arizona. We serve athletes ages 9 through Collegiate from September through April, building champions on the range and leaders in the community.

Our program is built on a foundation to instill discipline, gun safety, teamwork, self-confidence, sportsmanship, respect for authority, and other life values for our athletes.

Our Disciplines

Our certified shotgun shooting sports coaches train our athletes in all shotgun shooting sports disciplines:

🎯

Skeet

Fast-paced shooting at two moving targets from multiple stations, building speed and precision.

🎯

Trap

Targets launched away from the shooter, focusing on consistent form and reading downrange trajectories.

🎯

Sporting Clays

A variety of target presentations across multiple stations, developing versatility and adaptability.

🎯

ISkeet & Bunker Trap

We also offer the opportunity to explore the international disciplines of ISkeet and Bunker Trap.

Who We Are

Based at the world-renowned Ben Avery Clay Target Center, our team is made up of young athletes from across the Valley who share a passion for clay target shooting. Whether your child is a complete beginner or an experienced competitor, our program welcomes them with open arms.
