About the Team

Getting to know the Ben Avery Clay Crushers

Our Mission

The Ben Avery Clay Crushers is dedicated to developing young shooters in the disciplines of Skeet, Trap, and Sporting Clays. We provide a safe, supportive, and competitive environment where athletes ages 8–18 can learn the fundamentals of shotgun shooting, build confidence, and develop skills that last a lifetime.

Who We Are

Based at the world-renowned Ben Avery Shooting Range in Phoenix, Arizona, our team is made up of young athletes from across the Valley who share a passion for clay target shooting. Whether your child is a complete beginner or an experienced competitor, our program welcomes them with open arms.

What We Offer

🎯

Skeet

Fast-paced shooting at two moving targets from multiple stations, building speed and precision.

🎯

Trap

Targets launched away from the shooter, focusing on consistent form and reading downrange trajectories.

🎯

Sporting Clays

A variety of target presentations across multiple stations, developing versatility and adaptability.

Our Coaching Philosophy

We believe that great shooting starts with great fundamentals. Our coaching staff emphasizes proper gun handling, safety protocols, and consistent technique above all else. We don't just teach athletes how to hit targets — we teach them how to think about their shot, develop discipline, and approach each round with confidence and composure.

Beyond the range, we focus on character development. The skills our athletes learn — focus under pressure, respect for equipment and others, goal-setting, and resilience — translate to success in every area of their lives.
