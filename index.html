Our Disciplines

We train in three competitive shooting disciplines, each offering unique challenges and skills for athletes of all levels.

🎯

Skeet

Competitors shoot at two moving clay targets launched from high and low houses along eight shooting stations. Skeet requires quick reflexes, smooth gun handling, and the ability to track fast-crossing targets.

🎯

Trap

Shooters stand behind a single line and fire at targets launched away from them from a concealed machine. Trap emphasizes proper mount, consistent swing, and the ability to read target trajectory downrange.

🎯

Sporting Clays

Often called "golf with a shotgun," Sporting Clays features a variety of target trajectories along a course of stations. It develops adaptability and all-around shooting skills in realistic field conditions.

About the Team

The Ben Avery Clay Crushers is a youth shooting sports team based at the Ben Avery Clay Target Center in Phoenix, Arizona. We serve athletes ages 9 to Collegiate who are passionate about skeet, trap, and sporting clays.

Our program is built on a foundation of safety, skill development, and sportsmanship. Whether your child is picking up a shotgun for the first time or has been competing for years, there's a place for them on our team.

We believe shooting sports teach focus, discipline, and confidence that extend far beyond the range. Our coaches are dedicated to nurturing both the shooters and the leaders our athletes are becoming.
